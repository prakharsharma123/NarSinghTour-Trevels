Support
FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
Got a doubt? We're here to clear it up. Check out our most common inquiries below.
Do I need to pay a security deposit?
+

No! For all local rentals with a verified driver, we require absolutely zero security deposit. Only self-drive luxury models require a fully-refundable ₹5,000 hold.

Are fuel and tolls included in the price?
+

Fuel is not included in the standard daily rental unless you pick the "All-Inclusive Package". Tolls and interstate taxes are always charged at actuals during the trip.

What is your cancellation policy?
+

You can cancel any booking for free up to 24 hours before your pickup time. Cancellations within 24 hours incur a flat 20% cancellation fee.

Can I rent a car for interstate travel?
+

Absolutely. Most of our vehicles come with All-India Tourist Permits. Ensure you select 'Outstation' while searching to get interstate-ready cars.
